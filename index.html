Welcome to KARE...

We are 'Kenine Animal Rescue and Empaths' a non-profit organisation that works towards reducing human-animal conflict on the streets of Kolhapur, India since 2018.

We are dedicated to rescue and rehabilitate over hundreds of injured and sick street animals, run a Free Animal Hospital, conduct education and awareness sessions on peaceful urban co-existence.

We are also fighting cruelty against domestic and wild animals. And any violation of Prevention of cruelty to animals act.
